VISIT WEBSITE

This website was requested by a lawyer looking to completely overhaul their website. The goal was to create a brand new website with striking but simple visuals - nothing too flashy, yet still engaging. The challenge was creating a page that would give the potential client all of the information they would need to choose the lawyer to represent them, yet wouldn't overwhelm them with walls of text or legal jargon. Clients looking for lawyers typicaly are in a bind, so the website needed to be reassuring without definitively promising anything. Everything about the website was carefully chosen to exude confidence, be reassuring, and display key information such as awards and contact info up front. The result was a professional and responsive website that smoothly works on computers, tablets, and mobile devices.
